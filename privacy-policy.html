Privacy Policy of DayStride

This page is made to inform you about how DayStride ("we", "us") collects and uses information about you.

We reserve the right to update this page without notifying our users.


          
          
Information Collection

          
Our bot ("DayStride") collects some information about you and your servers, such as: Your server name and ID, in some cases channel ID's and if you suggest something or get blacklisted, your name and ID will be collected.

          
Our website ("https://daystride.github.io") does not collect any information about you.

          

          
Usage of your information

          
We collect this information to be able to contact/identify you for a better experience for you and other users.

          
Some of this information is used to provide you with different varieties of services.
